About

Bruno's Hotel offers a premium Roblox roleplay experience with modern rooms, fun activities, and an active community. Whether you're relaxing in your Suite or socializing in the lounge, it's always a good time at Bruno's!

Rules

Check-In & Check-Out:

  • Guests must check in during designated hours.
  • Check-out time is 11:00 AM. Please leave the room on time.

Room Behavior:

  • Keep rooms clean and tidy. No damaging furniture or decorations.
  • Respect the privacy of other guests.

No Refund Policy:

  • All payments are final. No refunds will be given for cancellations or early checkouts.

Common Areas:

  • Be respectful of shared spaces. Keep noise levels low.
  • No disruptive behavior in the lobby, pool, or other amenities.

NOTE: If any of these rules are broken, you will be kicked out or banned from the plot without explanation.
